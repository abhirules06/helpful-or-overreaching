================================================================
                  Q3 KICKOFF MEETING AGENDA
================================================================
Duration: 90 Minutes
Purpose:  Align the team on priorities, goals, and execution
          plan for Q3
----------------------------------------------------------------

1. WELCOME & OPENING REMARKS (5 min)
   - Meeting objectives and desired outcomes
   - Quick housekeeping / ground rules

----------------------------------------------------------------

2. Q2 RETROSPECTIVE: WINS & LESSONS LEARNED (15 min)
   - Key accomplishments from Q2
   - Missed targets and root causes
   - Carry-over items into Q3

----------------------------------------------------------------

3. Q3 COMPANY GOALS & STRATEGIC PRIORITIES (15 min)
   - OKRs / KPIs for Q3 (company-level)
   - How team goals ladder up to company objectives
   - Key themes and focus areas for the quarter

----------------------------------------------------------------

4. TEAM-LEVEL Q3 GOALS & COMMITMENTS (20 min)
   - Each function / workstream presents their Q3 goals
   - Success metrics and owners identified
   - Dependencies and cross-team needs flagged

----------------------------------------------------------------

5. RISKS, BLOCKERS & OPEN ISSUES (15 min)
   - Known risks that could impact Q3 delivery
   - Resource or capacity concerns
   - Items requiring leadership decisions or escalation

----------------------------------------------------------------

6. WAYS OF WORKING & OPERATING RHYTHM (10 min)
   - Cadence of standups, reviews, and check-ins for Q3
   - Communication norms and tools
   - Decision-making process for the quarter

----------------------------------------------------------------

7. ACTION ITEMS, OWNERS & NEXT STEPS (5 min)
   - Recap of decisions made in this meeting
   - Assign owners and due dates to open actions
   - Confirm date of first Q3 progress review

----------------------------------------------------------------

8. Q&A & OPEN DISCUSSION (5 min)
   - Team questions, concerns, or additions
   - Final alignment check before close

----------------------------------------------------------------

TOTAL TIME: 90 Minutes

================================================================
PRE-MEETING PREP FOR ATTENDEES:
  - Review Q2 results / metrics before the meeting
  - Come prepared with your team's proposed Q3 goals
  - Identify any dependencies on other teams in advance
================================================================
